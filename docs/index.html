About the Founders

Mehdi Allahyari
Full stack software engineer and AI engineer
  • Mehdi is a seasoned AI Researcher and Full-stack Software Engineer with an extensive background in AI/ML, large language models (LLMs), and natural language processing (NLP). Boasting over a decade of experience in both industry and academia, Mehdi brings a wealth of knowledge to the table.
  • Formerly a Computer Science professor at Georgia Southern University, Mehdi has made a significant impact in the field. His influential research papers have garnered over 2000 citations, and he has been recognized with a conference best paper award.
  • Among his recent accomplishments:

    • Developed a personalized recommendation system in the gaming industry.
    • Constructed large-scale intelligent search and question answering systems spanning domains such as finance, e-commerce, and human resources.
    • Established an NLP-as-a-service framework, empowering data scientists to seamlessly integrate their models for testing and demonstration.
Angelina Yang
Full stack data scientist and AI engineer
  • Angelina is a seasoned data scientist with a remarkable decade-long journey in the field. A two-time fellow with fast.ai and holder of ten patents in machine learning, Angelina has established herself as a prominent figure in the industry.
  • An adept practitioner in large language models (LLMs) since 2017, Angelina boasts a robust history of building and leading successful data science teams. Beyond her leadership prowess, she has demonstrated a keen commitment to knowledge-sharing as a Substack writer for two years and as the author of an insightful ebook on machine learning.
  • With a profound blend of expertise and a track record of innovation, Angelina stands as a valuable contributor to the world of data science.
